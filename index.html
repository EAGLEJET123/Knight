❓ How to Play
🗺️ Movement
• Click any visible tile to walk there
• WASD or Arrow Keys for step-by-step
• E = Auto-Explore   P = Potion   Q = Antidote
⚔️ Combat
• Roll a d20 to attack — higher is better!
• Nat 20 = Critical Hit   Nat 1 = Critical Miss
• Defend blocks 50% of incoming damage
• Each hero has a unique ability that costs MP
🎒 Items
• 🧪 Potions heal HP   📜 Scrolls deal magic damage
• 💣 Bombs deal heavy damage   🌿 Antidotes cure status
• ⚔️🛡️💍👢 Equipment boosts stats permanently
🗺️ Map Legend
• 📦 Treasure   💚 Healing Spring   🏪 Shop
• 💬 NPC   ❓ Secret Room   ✨ Random Event
• 🚪 Exit to next floor   👑 Boss monster
🏰 Goal
• Explore 5 floors and defeat all bosses!
• Reach the 🚪 exit on each floor to descend
• ESC = Pause   Game saves automatically
